Ultimate TODO List for Mandatory Greek Army Service

Background

  • I started my military army service in January 2021, at Litochoro - quite cold there.
  • I wanted to join KEPYES (the computer science batallion of the army and thus), had to gather some relevant certification for it.

To prepare

  • Send to KEPYES your application (postmaster@army.gr)
  • Make bootable persistent encrypted USB drive
    • GPG key
    • dotfiles
    • Pass
  • Update your passwords list - SD card
  • Measure your PowerBank capacity - How many times could it charge your phone --> 6
  • Revive your NAS storage so that you have access to it while away
  • Restrict card withdrawals from your banks - in case cards get stolen
  • Strip unnecessary cards from your wallet
    • Leave them at home

FAQ

  • Is it legal to continue working while doing my military service?
    • YES. See the army documents

One day before

  • Charge all your devices
    • Kindle
    • NOKIA
    • Main phone
  • Make sure NOKIA works with your nano sim cards
  • Fill phone storage with podcasts and books
  • Take a picture of the things you leave behind

Right before entering

  • Put your SIM card at the disposable phone - in case they get strict about using a phone that has a camera
