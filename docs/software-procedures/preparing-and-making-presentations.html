Hints

  • Sum up your presentation to up to 5 points Put them to a slide both at the beginning and at the end
  • Take a video of you multiple times before the presentation!! How do you look? Anything to improve?
  • Ask members of the audience after your talk (privately) or friends of yours before your presentation what points they remember, or *what slides were interesting!* If they don't remember or say something vague, then you've failed!
